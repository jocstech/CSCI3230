Overview

Hearthstone is a turn-based card game that is originated from Blizzard's World of Warcraft game.


Rules

The rules are as follows:

  • 1.) Each turn, both players gain 1 mana.
  • 2.) Players can only have 30 cards in their decks
  • 3.) A player can only hold 10 cards in their hands, any card drawn after that is discarded.
  • 4.) A player controlling a monster cannot attack its own minions.
  • 5.) When a player has a taunt on the board, minions and players can only attack the taunt minion. Spells and hero powers can go through the taunt minion.
  • 6.) Players can only have 2 of the same cards and 1 of any legendary. Decks will not be able to put 2 same copies of a legendary minion.

History

Hearthstone's contents is based on the original game: World of Warcraft. Each card has its own origin in the game, whether it is a common minion or a legendary minion or even a spell cast.
